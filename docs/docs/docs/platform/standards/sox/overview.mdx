# Overview

Sarbanes-Oxley Act (SOX) compliance is a yearly requirement for publicly traded companies in the U.S. It ensures that financial reporting is accurate, complete, and transparent. SOX compliance also protects investors and the public from accounting fraud.

## What does SOX compliance involve?

* Internal controls: Establishing and maintaining controls for financial records
* Data security: Safeguarding financial data, tracking breaches, and logging electronic records
* External audits: Having an independent audit firm review financial statements
* Accountability: Ensuring senior executives are responsible for the accuracy of financial reports
* Corporate governance: Adhering to administrative policies and ensuring proper governance
